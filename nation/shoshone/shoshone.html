Shoshone - Pocatello

Ability: Great Expanse

Founded Cities start with 4 additional tiles (previously 8) claimed. Units gain a +15% Combat Bonus in their own territory.

Unit: Pathfinder

Replaces the Scout. 27 hammers to produce (from 30, vs. 16).
8 Strength (vs. 5). When entering Ancient Ruins, the Pathfinder may select the benefit from the available pool. (This Unit upgrades into a Composite Bowman (instead of an Archer) from Ruins). Keeps the ability on upgrade.

Unit: Comanche Riders (unchanged)

Replaces Cavalry. 5 Movement (vs. 4) and costs less to produce (132 hammers vs. 150). Keeps the movement ability on upgrade.

Bias: None

Заменяет Разведчика. 27 единиц производства (вместо 30, против 16).

Юнит: Всадник команчей
